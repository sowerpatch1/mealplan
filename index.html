Week 1 Cholesterol-Friendly Meal Plan

This plan emphasizes cholesterol-supportive eating with high-fiber foods, lean proteins, legumes, whole grains, nuts/seeds, and unsaturated fats.

Protein values are approximate and aimed at supporting around 100g protein per day.

Educational use only. This is not medical advice. Adjust meals for your personal needs and clinician guidance.
